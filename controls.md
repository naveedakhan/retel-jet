## Keyboard Controls

### Flight Controls
- **W**: Throttle up
- **S**: Throttle down
- **Arrow Up**: Pitch up
- **Arrow Down**: Pitch down
- **Arrow Left**: Roll left
- **Arrow Right**: Roll right
- **A**: Yaw left
- **D**: Yaw right

### Camera & View
- **Space**: Cycle camera view (chase/cockpit/overview)
- **+ / - (or Numpad + / -)**: Zoom in/out while in overview camera
- **O**: Toggle overview camera orbit on/off

### Actions
- **B**: Toggle brake on/off
- **P**: Pause/unpause
- **R**: Reset jet position
- **L**: Toggle auto-level

## Tips
- In **mobile mode**, all keyboard controls are disabled to avoid conflicts
- The control mode toggle button is always visible and accessible
- Mobile controls are optimized for touch screens and work with both touch and mouse input
