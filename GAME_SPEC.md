# Retel Jet MVP Scope

## Goal
A simple browser-based 3D jet flying game with fun, stable controls.

## MVP Includes
- One jet (placeholder mesh).
- One simple world (flat ocean + sky).
- Keyboard flight controls.
- HUD with speed, altitude, and throttle.

## Not Doing (Yet)
- Weapons, missiles, guns.
- Enemies or AI.
- Missions, scoring, or objectives.
- Terrain, islands, or buildings.
- Multiplayer or networking.
- Sound effects or music.
- Joystick/gamepad input.
